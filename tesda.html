TESDA QUALIFICATIONS

VISUAL GRAPHIC DESIGN NC III

(501 hours)

This qualification is designed to enhance the knowledge, skills and attitudes of a trainee/student on core competencies such as:

  • Develop design studies, Create vector graphics using a graphics application, Create raster graphics using a graphics application, Develop designs for print media, Develop designs for electronic media, Develop designs for product packaging, Design designs for booth and product/window display in accordance with the Industry standards.

It includes common competencies such as:

  • Apply Quality Standards, Perform Computer Operations.
